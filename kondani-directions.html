Kondani — three original directions

Not a Tinder copy. Each one is built around the Intent idea and rooted in Malawi. Pick one (or mix).

The big idea first

Kondani means "love one another" in Chichewa. The thing no big app does well: people post what they actually want to do right now — coffee at Mamma Mia, a Mulanje hike, Afrobeats tonight — and others join. It's dating built around real plans, real meetups, not endless swiping. That's our wedge. Below are three visual personalities for that idea. The screen shown is the same Intents feed in each, so you can judge the vibe fairly.

My honest take as your engineer: for a trust-driven dating app in a smaller market, I'd lead with A — Lake of Stars for the premium, safe, romantic feel, OR B — Warm Heart if you want to stand out hard and signal "serious & wholesome." None of these copy Tinder — the swipe becomes a secondary "Discover" tab, while the home screen is Plans / Intents, which is genuinely ours.
