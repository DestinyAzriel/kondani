✦ Lake of Stars

Kondani — the full app, one design language

Every screen in the Lake of Stars palette: deep lake-night teal, warm gold starlight, lagoon-teal accents. Swipe is home (the Discover tab); Plans — post what you're up to and let people join — is a distinctive second tab, plus Daily Picks for curated matches. Login is a simple phone number + one-time code. Premium is priced in MWK via Airtel/TNM. Phone frames show mobile; everything scales to a centered desktop column.

Still static mockups — no logic wired yet. On your approval, the build order is: (1) fix the auth/token inconsistencies in the current code, (2) rebuild the UI screen-by-screen into this language, (3) scaffold the backend (Node + free Postgres) and the Telegram OTP bot, (4) wire each screen to live data, (5) deploy to Render.

Tell me any tweaks — different gold/teal balance, rounder vs sharper cards, icons instead of emoji — and I'll adjust before we touch code.
